How can I get there?

We recommend you book your travel to the ceremony in advance using Trent Cars, which is a local taxi company that provides good prices. For returning back to your accomodation, it also accepts on-the-spot orders.

Public transport is not recommended. Nottingham's public transport is limited and fairly expensive for what it is. If you do wish to use public transport, you can use Google Maps to find your route, but be aware that exact change is required.

Alternatively, there is also Uber in Nottingham!

Parking spaces are available at the ceremony and reception location. We advise you park at the reception location (school) so that you would not need to move the vehicle when we continue the event at the reception location.
