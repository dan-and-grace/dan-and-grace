Postponement

Dear Guests,

We hope that you are all well and managing during this challenging time.

We have an important message to tell you regarding our wedding date. While we are hopeful that the global situation will continue to improve in the coming months, there is still some uncertainty as to what the situation will be on our planned wedding day with regards to protective measures such as social distancing and restrictions on travelling. In order for us all to be able to look forward to the event without having to worry about these things, we have decided to put our wedding plans on hold until all safety restrictions have been lifted. This means that our wedding and reception will NOT be taking place on 24th October 2020.

There is no need to be disappointed, because we will be rescheduling the wedding and reception to a different date, which we will decide at the time when we are sure that the pandemic will no longer pose a problem. We hope that this will not be too far in the future, but we will of course be letting you know the new date with enough time in advance to prepare, particularly for any overseas guests who need to buy plane tickets.

Please note that, before making the decision to postpone the day, we had already posted our formal invitations for the original date. Due to delays in air mail, it is likely that this may not have arrived with you by the time you receive this message. If this is the case, please disregard any invitation you receive from us showing the date 24th October 2020, and await a new invitation in the near future showing a new date.

We are so very much looking forward to seeing you on our new wedding date; we have a wonderful day planned and we are glad that this postponement means that we will all be able to enjoy it without any health or travel restrictions getting in the way. Stay tuned for a new invitation from us, and please do get in touch if you have any questions. Our email addresses are daniel.bartley@hotmail.co.uk and 27grace@gmail.com.

Lots of love,

Dan & Grace
